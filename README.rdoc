#Secrets#

##About##

Secrets is an app that allows users to post their secrets anonymously from anywhere in the world. Secrets aren't hidden behind a signup wall, so all secrets are available for anyone to view, copy and share. The app is intentionally not RESTful--secrets can be created and rendered, but they can't be updated or destroyed. In other words, be careful what you post--because it will be on "Secrets" forever!
